Why Waste Management?

  • Waste management is crucial for preserving our planet's health and ensuring a sustainable future.
  • Millions of tons of waste end up in landfills and oceans yearly, causing severe environmental damage.
  • Proper waste management reduces pollution, conserves resources, and minimizes greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Responsible disposal protects ecosystems, improves public health, and creates a cleaner world.
  • Remember: Every small action counts — from recycling one bottle to segregating waste at home.
